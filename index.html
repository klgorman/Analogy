A Framework for Getting Started

Beginning the AI conversation in your organization

Artificial intelligence doesn't enter a company through software. It enters through people — their questions, their fears, their curiosity, and their judgment. What follows is a proven process for starting that conversation well.

Three Pillars
01
Fluency

Build a shared language

Before any strategic decisions can be made, leadership and their teams need a common foundation. This means leveling up on the basics — not the hype, not the fear, but the actual landscape — so that everyone in the room is working from the same understanding. Without shared language, AI conversations stall before they start.

Executive Talk Day of Learning Curated Asset Library
02
Philosophy

Align AI to your values

The tools you adopt will reflect — and reinforce — who you are as an organization. Bringing critical decision-makers together to examine how your mission, vision, and values should shape your AI choices is not a soft exercise. It is the most important strategic work you will do. This is where you decide what AI will and won't be asked to do in your community.

Leadership Workshop Values Mapping Decision Framework
03
Proficiency

Build real capability across the organization

Proficiency isn't a training event. It is an ongoing culture of learning, built through education, peer-to-peer connection, and the kind of genuine comfort with tools that only comes from use. This pillar ensures the conversation doesn't stop at the executive level — it moves through the entire community and grows over time.

What comes next
Perception The horizon beyond proficiency

When your organization begins to see what it couldn't see before

Organizations that move through Fluency, Philosophy, and Proficiency reach a threshold moment — the point at which AI stops being something you adopt and becomes something you wield. Perception is the capacity to look at your own data, your own operations, your own community with new eyes: to recognize patterns, surface hidden opportunities, and build tools designed specifically for who you are. This is bespoke intelligence. It is not available at the beginning of the journey. It is what the journey is for.
